About W&K Cabinet

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

W&K Cabinet is a premier custom cabinet maker serving Port Elizabeth and surrounding areas (Uitenhage, Despatch, Motherwell, Newton Park, Summerstrand, Walmer) with exceptional woodwork solutions. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in creating beautiful, functional cabinetry that enhances your living spaces.

Our team of skilled craftsmen combines traditional techniques with modern technology to deliver cabinets that are not only stunning but built to last. We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to quality in every project we undertake.
